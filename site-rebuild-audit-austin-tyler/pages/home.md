## Original Body Copy *(verbatim — preserve exactly)*

**Bio (intro):**
> I'm Austin Tyler, a Nashville-based photographer and videographer creating visual stories rooted in authenticity. From live performances to behind-the-scenes, promotional content, and portraits - I focus on capturing energy, emotion, and presence as they naturally unfold.

> At the core of my work is connection. I value building real relationships with the people I work with, earning trust, and creating imagery that reflects who they truly are—not just what they do. My goal is to document moments as they're lived and help artists, brands, and creatives tell stories that last.

**Showcase collection:**
> Live performance photography is about capturing the energy, emotion, and movement that happen in real time on stage. I focus on candid moments, crowd interaction, and the raw presence of the artist as the performance unfolds. From stage lighting to spontaneous expressions, every image tells the story of the show as it was felt, not just how it looked. This style creates authentic visuals that connect fans to the experience. It's not posed — it's alive.

**Promotional COLLECTION:**
> Promotional content for artists is about visually translating the sound, identity, and emotion behind the music. I create photo and video assets that align with an artist's brand and support releases, tours, and marketing campaigns. From concept to execution, each piece is designed to feel authentic while standing out online. The goal is content that tells a story and builds connection before the music is even played. It's branding with purpose, not just visuals.

**Studio COLLECTION:**
> Studio sessions are a controlled, creative environment designed to bring an artist's vision to life. I focus on clean visuals, intentional lighting, and direction that feels natural, not forced. These sessions are ideal for branding, press, and promotional use across platforms. Every detail is considered to create polished images that still feel authentic. It's where concept meets clarity.
