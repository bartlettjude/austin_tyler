# 12 — Rebuild Handoff Summary

## What the website is
**austintylerartistry.com** — the portfolio + booking site for **Austin Tyler**, a Nashville-based **photographer & videographer for music artists** (live performances, promotional/release content, studio sessions). Built on Squarespace 7.1 from a template, recently launched (~early 2026), and currently **half-finished**: strong work and strong brand copy wrapped in leftover template placeholders, duplicate/junk pages, broken auto-SEO, and a hidden booking path.

## What content was collected
- **All page copy, verbatim** for every page (bio, three collection write-ups, all five package descriptions + inclusions + prices, both contact forms' copy, store fine print, footer). → `03_CONTENT_INVENTORY.md` + `pages/`.
- **All metadata** (titles, descriptions, OG, canonical) for every page. → `08_…`.
- **All links, socials, contacts, CTAs** → `05_…`, `07_…`, and CSVs in `data/`.
- **Navigation + full sitemap** (including orphan/junk pages) → `06_…`.

## What assets were collected
- **35 real images downloaded** (2500px) into organized `assets/images/` folders: logo, 26 portfolio photos, 2 team/BTS shots, 3 promo covers, 2 product photos, 1 live-stage banner.
- **6 self-hosted videos documented** (URLs/IDs/durations) in `assets/videos/VIDEO_MANIFEST.md` — masters to be collected from client.
- Template/stock/spacer junk documented but not downloaded.
- Everything indexed in `data/all-images.csv` with alt text + recommendations.

## Most important pages
Home · Photos (portfolio) · Services (packages/pricing) · Information=Contact (the inquiry form). These four carry the brand and the conversion.

## Most important CTAs
"Book a session" (to be added everywhere), the inquiry form submit, and package "Book" buttons (to be added). Currently the only real CTAs are "View full gallery →" and a mis-targeted "About Austin →".

## Best existing content to PRESERVE
- The **bio** and the **three collection descriptions** (genuinely good, on-voice copy).
- The **photography** (the site's biggest asset) and the **promo covers** (proof of real artist clients).
- The package model + pricing ($75–$250) and the lines **"available to tour"** / **"based in Nashville, available for travel."**
- The first-person, authenticity-driven **voice** and the lowercase **"austintylerartistry."** wordmark.

## Weakest parts of the current site
- Template leftovers + junk/duplicate pages (Ecosystem, /sereno duplicate, "Store 2", "Your Site Title").
- No About page; "About Austin" → a form.
- Hidden store, no hero CTA, no package booking buttons.
- Wrong/auto-generated SEO ("Austin and Tyler" cities; "Sofia Parazi"; title typo) and missing H1s.
- Multiple typos; inconsistent capitalization; stock/spacer images.

## Recommended new direction
A **dark, cinematic, image-led** rebuild with a real headline + Book CTA, organized Live/Promo/Studio galleries, a genuine About, named-client social proof, and a single clear booking path — same authentic Nashville-music-storyteller soul, but finished, consistent, and conversion-focused. Full structure in `11_…`; voice/visuals in `09_…`.

## Missing client materials (collect before/early in rebuild)
Headshot · expanded bio · real testimonials · permission to name artists · master video files + a horizontal reel · vector logo + favicon · final pricing/deliverables (esp. $250 combo) · commerce-vs-inquiry decision · contact-form fields · any other socials/press.
